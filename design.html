Full Stack Designer

I enjoy the full process from UX Research through to "Front of the Frontend" Coding.

Although, I have the most fun upfront with co-creation and rapid iteration.

It's a weird term, but in an environment dominated by Full Stack Devs it makes sense.

  • I research User Problems
  • I design solutions with pen, paper and prototypes
  • I test and iterate those designs
  • I code them (at least the looky touchy bits)
